Our Love Story

2011

We meet each other

We met five years ago on the elevator of Ellis's San Francisco apartment building. Ellis casually asked if Lauren would like to stop by for a beer sometime.
2011

Our first date

A few months later, we went on our first date to Smuggler's Cove. We discovered we shared a passion for music, cycling, and travel.
2011

Early Days

We grew close as we explored the city and its beautiful surroundings together. Before long we were inseparable!
2012

Long distance

We navigated a year of long distance (after Lauren returned to DC), with frequent trips back and forth to see each other.
2013

New Adventures

We decided to take the plunge and move in together in Ellis's studio apartment in San Francisco. Tess came along, too! Soon we found the perfect apartment for the three of us, where we've spent the last few happy years together.
2016

Our happy engagement

Ellis proposed on the beach while we were vacationing in Tulum, Mexico, and Lauren said yes!
